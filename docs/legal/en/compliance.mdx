# Legal Compliance & Regulatory Information

**Effective Date:** January 15, 2025

ResearchEthics Platform is committed to operating in full compliance with applicable laws, regulations, and industry standards. This document outlines our compliance framework and legal obligations.

## 1. Data Protection Compliance

### 1.1 GDPR Compliance (EU/EEA Users)

We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the European Economic Area:

**Legal Basis for Processing:**
- Contract performance
- Legitimate interests
- Legal obligations
- Consent (where required)

**User Rights Under GDPR:**
- Right to access personal data
- Right to rectification
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object
- Rights related to automated decision-making

**Data Protection Officer:**  
Email: dpo@researchethics.com

**EU Representative:**  
[To be appointed if required under GDPR Article 27]

### 1.2 CCPA Compliance (California, USA)

For California residents, we comply with the California Consumer Privacy Act:

**Rights Under CCPA:**
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
- Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
- Right to deletion
- Right to non-discrimination

**Notice:**  
We do NOT sell personal information.

**Designated Contact:**  
Email: ccpa@researchethics.com

### 1.3 POPIA Compliance (South Africa)

We comply with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) for South African users.

### 1.4 Cameroon Data Protection Law

As a Cameroon-based platform, we comply with:
- Law No. 2010/012 of 21 December 2010 on cybersecurity and cybercrime
- Any applicable data protection regulations in Cameroon

## 7. Privacy and Communications Compliance

### 7.1 Email Marketing

We comply with anti-spam laws:

**CAN-SPAM Act (USA):**
- Clear identification of commercial emails
- Accurate header information
- Opt-out mechanism in every email
- Honor opt-out requests within 10 days

**GDPR (EU/EEA):**
- Explicit consent for marketing emails
- Easy unsubscribe process
- No pre-checked consent boxes

### 7.2 SMS/Mobile Communications

If we implement SMS notifications:
- Opt-in required
- Clear identification of sender
- Opt-out instructions provided
- Compliance with TCPA (USA) and similar laws
