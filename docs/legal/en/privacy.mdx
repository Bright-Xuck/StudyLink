# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 15, 2025

ZenithScholar Platform ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use our e-learning platform.

By using the Platform, you consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

**Account Registration:**
- Full name
- Email address
- Password (encrypted)
- Phone number (optional)
- Department/Faculty affiliation (optional)

**Profile Information:**
- Profile photo (if uploaded)
- Bio or description
- Academic interests
- Professional affiliations

**Course Activity:**
- Quiz responses and scores
- Assignment submissions
- Course progress and completion data
- Certificate information

**Payment Information:**
- Transaction details
- Payment method information (processed securely by third-party payment processors)
- Billing address
- Purchase history

**Communications:**
- Support inquiries and correspondence
- Feedback and survey responses
- Forum posts and comments

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you access the Platform, we automatically collect:

**Technical Data:**
- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Device information (type, operating system)
- Screen resolution
- Time zone and locale settings

**Usage Data:**
- Pages visited and time spent
- Click patterns and navigation paths
- Search queries
- Referral sources
- Session duration

**Cookies and Tracking:**
- Session cookies for authentication
- Preference cookies for settings
- Analytics cookies for usage patterns
- See our [Cookie Policy](/cookies) for details

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

- Provide services to you
- Comply with legal obligations
- Resolve disputes
- Enforce our agreements

**Retention Periods:**

- **Active Accounts:** Data retained while account is active
- **Inactive Accounts:** Deleted after 3 years of inactivity
- **Transaction Records:** Retained for 7 years for legal/tax purposes
- **Marketing Data:** Retained until you withdraw consent

You can request deletion of your account and data at any time (subject to legal retention requirements).

## 6. Your Data Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

### 6.1 Access and Portability

- **Right to Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Data Portability:** Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format

### 6.2 Correction and Deletion

- **Right to Rectification:** Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Right to Erasure:** Request deletion of your personal data

### 6.3 Processing Limitations

- **Right to Restrict Processing:** Limit how we process your data
- **Right to Object:** Object to processing based on legitimate interests

### 6.4 Consent Withdrawal

- **Right to Withdraw Consent:** Withdraw consent for marketing or optional data collection

### 6.5 Complaint Rights

- **Right to Lodge a Complaint:** File a complaint with your local data protection authority

**To exercise your rights, contact us at:** privacy@zenithscholar.com

## 7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

### 7.1 Technical Safeguards

- SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
- Encrypted password storage (bcrypt hashing)
- Secure database access controls
- Regular security audits and updates
- Firewall protection

### 7.2 Organizational Safeguards

- Access controls and authentication
- Employee training on data protection
- Confidentiality agreements with staff
- Incident response procedures

### 7.3 Limitations

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
